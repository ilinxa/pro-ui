#### F-02 — Live visual walkthrough owed

- **Severity:** 🔹 Low
- **Dimension:** Design system
- **Location:** `/components/team-progress-bar-01`
- **Observed:** `pnpm build` generates the detail page and the demo compiles, but no browser render was confirmed in this headless environment (the project's de-facto visual gate).
- **Suggested fix:** Walk through the demo tabs post-deploy (fill geometry, ticks alignment, fraction readout, the `total === 0` 0% bar, reduced-motion).

#### F-03 — `fraction` dev-warn wording in the both-supplied edge

- **Severity:** 🔹 Low
- **Dimension:** Component internals
- **Location:** `lib/resolve-progress.ts` `resolveLabelFormat`
- **Observed:** When `value` **and** `milestones` are both supplied with `labelFormat="fraction"`, `total` is null (value-mode), so the readout correctly falls back to percent — but the warning reads "requires `milestones`" even though milestones *were* supplied (value just took precedence). Behavior is correct; only the message is slightly misleading. The both-supplied case already emits its own dedicated warning.
- **Suggested fix:** v0.1.1 — soften the message or skip the fraction warn when milestones are present-but-overridden.

#### F-04 — Final tick centers on the right edge

- **Severity:** 🔹 Low
- **Dimension:** Design system
- **Location:** `parts/team-progress-bar-track.tsx` (tick `insetInlineStart` `((i+1)/n)*100%` + `translateX(-50%)`)
- **Observed:** The last milestone notch sits at 100% and is centered on the bar's right edge, so its outer half renders just past the track end (the overlay wrapper isn't clipped). Reads as an end-cap; harmless but slightly asymmetric vs interior notches.
- **Suggested fix:** v0.1.1 — optionally inset the tick band by half a notch width, or special-case the terminal notch.
