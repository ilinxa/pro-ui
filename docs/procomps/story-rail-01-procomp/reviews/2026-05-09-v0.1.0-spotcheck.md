## Findings (1)

### F-01 — `onItemClick(item, index)` is the 4th occurrence of positional-callback pattern across the library

- **Severity:** 🔹 Low (individually) — but **collectively confirms F-cross-NN escalation per audit-systematic-scope memory**
- **Dimension:** 14 — Cross-component coherence (and Dim 2 — Public API)
- **Location:** `src/registry/components/data/story-rail-01/types.ts` (`onItemClick?: (item: StoryRailItem, index: number) => void`) + `story-rail-01.tsx:133` (passed through to thumbnails)
- **Observed:** 4th occurrence of the positional-callback shape across reviewed components:
  1. `grid-layout-news-01` F-02 (s8) — `renderItem(item, slot)`
  2. `content-card-news-01` F-01 (s10) — `onClick(item, event)`
  3. `project-card-01` F-01 (s11) — `onClick(project, mouseEvent)`
  4. `story-rail-01` F-01 (s12) — `onItemClick(item, index)`
  
  Per the audit-systematic-scope memory: **4 occurrences = systemic pattern**, not an individual-component nit. Ready for F-cross-NN escalation at sweep close (s13). Per Dim 2 review-guide: *"positional callbacks are a versioning trap."* All 4 will be breaking-change candidates in v0.2 to convert to object-shape `(args: { item, ... })`.
- **Suggested fix:** v0.1.x: leave each as-is per individual-component reviews. **At sweep close (session 13):** evaluate as F-cross-NN candidate — propose a library-wide migration plan to object-shape callbacks in v0.2 across all 4 components (and audit Tier 1 for additional shapes — workspace, kanban, flow-canvas, rich-card, etc. are likely candidates).
